hi! i'm kathryn. you can call me katie.

I am a Johnson Scholar at Washington and Lee University ('24) studying Computer Science and Philosophy with a minor in Poverty and Human Capability Studies. Informed and inspired by my history with mental health and addictive, exploitative social media algorithms, I am passionate about eradicating algorithmic injustice from the technologies that increasingly shape our individual and collective futures.

Currently, I am conducting research with Amy X. Zhang, Quan Ze (Jim) Chen, and Leijie Wang in the University of Washington’s Social Futures Lab, where I am working to develop and pilot an interactive, complex word filter that leverages LLM support to help YouTube creators combat harassment online. Last summer, I conducted social media research with Daniel Klug and Ella Steen in Carnegie Mellon University’s MINT (Media, Interaction & Technology) Lab, where I co-authored the first academic publication highlighting the phenomenon of "algospeak."

My involvement in Google's Computer Science Research Mentorship Program, Women in Technology and Science (Computer Science Co-Chair), and the University of Washington's AccessComputing team strengthens my commitment to supporting marginalized communities and promoting diversity in computing.

On campus, I serve as the President of Nabors Service League, Co-President of the Volunteer Venture Program , and Vice President of The Campus Kitchen, through which I organize, lead, and engage in volunteer opportunities in Washington and Lee's local community. As a Bonner Scholar, I participate in leadership development programs that help me engage respectfully in community efforts, like redesigning the outdated website of my university's local community foundation. Being recognized as a Finalist for the national Truman Scholarship highlights my dedication to leveraging my technical skills for social good.

In my spare time, I enjoy guiding meditations, weightlifting, public speaking, reading political philosophy texts, jigsaw puzzling (avidly), and Paleo baking.
