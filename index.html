Kathryn Yurechko About Research Writing

hi! i'm kathryn

I do social computing research in hopes of making people nicer to each other on the internet. I truly believe that better online communication is the key to building a more compassionate society. I’m currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Washington, advised by Professor Amy X. Zhang.

I used to write the League of Legends patch notes while working at Riot Games, which have been read by millions of players worldwide. Before that, I was a Program Manager at Microsoft designing software for enterprise mobility and security. I graduated from Brown University in 2018 with a double major in Computer Science and Modern Culture & Media.
